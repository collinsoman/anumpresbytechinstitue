About Us

A Legacy of Skill and Innovation.

Anum Presbyterian Technical Institute (APTI), located in the Asuogyaman District of the Eastern Region, is a premier institution dedicated to vocational and technical education. Our schoolâ€™s motto, "Service and Sacrifice," guides our commitment to training disciplined and skilled professionals who are ready to contribute to Ghana's development.

We provide a dynamic learning environment supported by workshops and state-of-the-art labs for all our courses. Our programs are designed to be hands-on, ensuring that every student graduates with the practical expertise and confidence needed to excel in their chosen trade.

Our Technical Programs

We offer specialized, hands-on training to prepare students for successful careers in skilled trades.

Computer & Software Technology

Learn hardware repair, network administration, and software development in a practical environment.

Plumbing & Gas Fitting Technology

Master the installation and maintenance of modern plumbing and gas systems for a high-demand trade.

Fashion Design

From sketching to garment production, this course covers all aspects of the fashion industry.

Catering & Hospitality

Develop culinary expertise and learn the fundamentals of hotel and restaurant management.

Business Secretarial

Master administrative skills, office management, and business communication for a corporate role.

Building & Construction Technology

Get hands-on training in construction techniques, from foundation work to advanced building systems.

Electrical Engineering Technology

Learn to design, install, and maintain electrical systems and power generation for a dynamic career.
